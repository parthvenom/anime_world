Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular anime series based on the manga by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer after ingesting a cursed object. He joins forces with other sorcerers to defeat cursed spirits and protect humanity. Known for its intense action, captivating storyline, and well-developed characters, "Jujutsu Kaisen" has garnered widespread acclaim and a dedicated fanbase since its release.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan" is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity resides within massive walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. Known for its intense action, complex characters, and intricate plot twists, "Attack on Titan" has become one of the most popular and influential anime series of recent years.

Solo leveling

"Solo Leveling" is a South Korean web novel turned manhwa (comic) written by Chu-Gong. It follows Sung Jin-Woo, a low-ranked hunter in a world where gates to dungeons full of monsters have appeared. After a fateful encounter, Jin-Woo gains the ability to level up like a game character, embarking on a journey of power and revenge. With its captivating storyline, breathtaking artwork, and intense action scenes, "Solo Leveling" has garnered a massive following worldwide.

Demon Slayer

"Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" is a Japanese anime adapted from the manga by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, and his sister Nezuko is turned into one. Tanjiro embarks on a quest to avenge his family and cure Nezuko, encountering allies and powerful demons along the way. Renowned for its stunning animation, compelling characters, and emotional storytelling, "Demon Slayer" has become a cultural phenomenon both in Japan and internationally.
